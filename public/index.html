Demo Guide

What this app proves

A product demo of a Financial Guardian & Optimizer that keeps the dashboard clean while proving two outcomes: credit protection (high‑stakes obligations) and savings discovery (bundle duplicates).

Purpose

What the demo is designed to show

  • Consent‑first tracking: fixed obligations appear only after the user adds them via Fetch.
  • Credit protection: highlight high‑impact due‑soon items and surface a simple safety score.
  • Optimization: flag OTT duplicates already included in telco bundles and estimate savings.
  • Control‑gap aware actions: adapt actions based on payment rail and MCC (bank‑only vs revocable).

Flow

60‑second walkthrough

  1. Open Subscriptions → call out the two top metrics.
  2. In Fixed Obligations, note it’s intentionally empty pre‑consent.
  3. Click Fetch my bills → review detected bills with impact tags.
  4. Click Add selected bills to Guardian → cards appear and the score updates.
  5. Open Lifestyle Subs → show bundle duplicates and “Switch to bundle”.

What to call out

Talking points

Credit protection

High‑impact items (EMI/insurance) due soon raise a CIBIL protection alert. The score is a simple proxy for “risk window visibility”, not a real bureau score.

Optimization

The app flags subscriptions already included in bundles (Jio/Airtel) as potential savings and nudges users to switch safely.

Control gaps

Some obligations can’t be canceled via third parties. Actions adapt based on payment rail and MCC (e.g., loan repayments are “Manage via bank”).

Demo note: This app uses simulated data and flows for clarity. It does not connect to real BBPS/AA rails.

Business Pitch

Why this wins

India’s bill ecosystem is fragmented across banks, billers, autopay rails and telco bundles. This product unifies visibility, risk protection, and optimization into one consent-led experience.

Problem

Missed high‑stakes bills are costly

EMIs and insurance are spread across mandates and billers. Users lack a single place to see what’s due soon, and late payments can impact credit outcomes.

Solution

Guardian + Optimizer

Fetch obligations (BBPS/AA-style), track only what the user selects, highlight CIBIL-risk items, and surface savings from duplicate subscriptions already included in telco plans.

Moat

Control-gap intelligence

Actions are rail-aware: UPI AutoPay revoke flows, card mandate redirects, and bank-only management for loan MCCs. This reduces failed attempts and builds user trust.

Go-to-market

Partner-led distribution

Launch via lenders, insurers, fintech apps, and telcos: reduce delinquency risk, improve renewal rates, and offer a better “financial health” experience without handling money directly.

Revenue

SaaS + success fees

B2B2C pricing for partners plus optional success-based savings conversion (e.g., bundle switch guidance) and premium credit-protection alerts.

Compliance-first

Consent + view-only posture

The product emphasizes explicit user consent and view-only data access patterns, aligning with regulated rails while keeping the demo clear and credible.
